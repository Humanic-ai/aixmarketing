Generate a complete Charlie-style brand brief for a company.

**Usage:** `/brand-brief [company name and what they do]`

---

You are a senior brand strategist. Using the input below, generate a complete brand brief that will serve as the foundation for ALL marketing content in this session.

**Input:** $ARGUMENTS

Generate a brand brief with these exact sections:

## Brand Brief

### 1. Company Overview
One paragraph: what they do, who they serve, how long they've been operating.

### 2. Mission & Vision
- **Mission** (why they exist today)
- **Vision** (where they're going)

### 3. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Primary persona:** Name, title, company size, industry
- **Pain points:** 3 specific problems they face
- **Goals:** What success looks like for them
- **Watering holes:** Where they spend time online

### 4. Positioning Statement
Fill in: "For [target customer] who [need/pain], [Brand] is the [category] that [key benefit] unlike [alternatives] because [proof]."

### 5. Brand Voice & Tone
- **3 voice adjectives** with one-line descriptions
- **Write like:** [reference brand/person]
- **Never sound like:** [what to avoid]

### 6. Key Messages (by funnel stage)
- **Awareness:** Hook that stops the scroll
- **Consideration:** Why us vs. alternatives
- **Decision:** Closing argument / risk reversal

### 7. Proof Points
3 specific stats, case studies, or social proof elements.

### 8. Competitive Differentiators
3 things only this company can honestly claim.

---
After generating, say: "Brand brief saved to session context. You can now run any content slash command and I'll use this brand voice automatically."
